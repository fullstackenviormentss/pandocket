# Northwest Ordinance of 1787

> This ordinance, passed by the Second Continental Congress, prohibited slavery and involuntary servitude in the Northwest Territory, which later became the states of Ohio, Indiana, Illinois, Michigan, and Wisconsin.

## Questions
